A Journey of Discipline and Excellence

My martial arts journey began in 2014 with the practice of Vovinam Viet Vo Dao. Currently training at the Vovinam Occitan club, this discipline has become a cornerstone of my personal and professional balance. Throughout my practice, I have had the privilege of being mentored by distinguished experts, including Master Cyril Sandre (5th Dang) and Instructor Fabien Laforet (4th Dang). Their rigorous training and demand for excellence have pushed me to surpass my limits, providing me with the technical proficiency and mental fortitude required to compete at high levels. Consequently, I have successfully participated in numerous national and international competitions, experiences that have taught me how to perform under pressure and embrace challenges. Beyond physical performance, Vovinam is vital to me for its profound philosophical dimension. The values of humility, perseverance, and the harmony between strength and flexibility ("Cương Nhu Phối Triển") are principles I strive to embody every day, both in the dojo and in my engineering career.
